Our Partners

Club Atlantique

Club Atlantique is a sports association dedicated to supporting and empowering people with disabilities. The club provides inclusive athletic programs designed to promote physical well-being, confidence, and social integration.

Boukhalef Social Integration Complex

The Boukhalef Social Integration Complex is a social-oriented facility that works to provide shelter and reintegration for vulnerable individuals. It accommodates in particular:

  • Boys aged 6 to 18 living in difficult situations or on the streets,
  • Elderly people (men and women) aged 55 and over.

The complex also develops educational projects focused on non-formal education, literacy, and vocational training, thereby contributing to improving living conditions and supporting the social reintegration of its beneficiaries.
